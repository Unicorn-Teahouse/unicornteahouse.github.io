When are you open?


We are open for special events on weekday and Saturday nights and Sunday all day. We are also open during the day for salon and spa services, smaller pop-up art gallery hours, community gatherings, and tours. Want a full tour of our space? We highly recommend that you schedule one in advance by emailing hello@unicornteahouse.com.

Where are you located?


We are located in the heart of Buckhead Atlanta near the intersection of Peachtree Rd and Piedmont Rd.

3222 Peachtree Rd NE
Atlanta GA 30305

Do you have accessible facilities?

Our entrance is located on the left side of the building and is at ground level. We also have accessible restrooms. If you have any accessibility questions or needs, please reach out to us at hello@unicornteahouse.com.

Where do I park?


Free parking is available in the front and rear of the building off of Peachtree Rd NE.
